# How to Make a Parking QR Code That Protects Your Privacy

Leaving your phone number on the dashboard is the old way. It exposes your personal information to strangers, spammers, and anyone who walks by your car. A **parking QR code** solves this by letting people scan a code to notify you—without ever seeing your phone number.

In this guide, you'll learn how to make a parking QR code, what features matter, and why a privacy-first service like **MoveCar** is the best option in 2026.

## Why Use a Parking QR Code?

- **Privacy**: Your phone number stays hidden.
- **Convenience**: Scanners don't need to install an app.
- **Safety**: You control when and how people contact you.
- **Location aware**: See exactly where your car is being requested.

## What to Look For in a Parking QR Code Service

1. **No phone number exposure**: The scanner should never see your number.
2. **No app required for scanners**: A browser page is best.
3. **Multiple notification channels**: Email alone is easy to miss.
4. **Location sharing**: Know where the request is coming from.
5. **Spam protection**: Delay or rate limits reduce abuse.
